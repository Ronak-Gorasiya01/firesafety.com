Using Your Fire Detection System

Understanding the System

The system monitors environmental conditions using IoT sensors and cameras. Ensure these devices are installed in high-risk areas such as kitchens or storage rooms.

Regular Maintenance Tips

Check the sensors regularly for dust or debris that might affect performance. Update software periodically to ensure optimal functionality.

Emergency Procedures

In case of a fire alarm, evacuate immediately and follow the evacuation plan. Ensure all family members are aware of emergency protocols.
