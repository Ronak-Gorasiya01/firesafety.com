Step-by-Step Process

Step 1: Data Collection

Our system uses IoT sensors and cameras to continuously monitor environmental conditions such as temperature, humidity, smoke, and flame patterns.

Step 2: AI Analysis

The collected data is analyzed in real-time using advanced AI algorithms like YOLOv8 and Haar Cascade Classifier to detect fire-related anomalies.

Step 3: Alerts and Notifications

If a fire hazard is detected, the system immediately sends alerts to users via mobile apps, SMS, or email notifications.

Step 4: Automated Extinguishing

The system activates automated extinguishing mechanisms such as sprinklers or drones equipped with fire suppression tools.
